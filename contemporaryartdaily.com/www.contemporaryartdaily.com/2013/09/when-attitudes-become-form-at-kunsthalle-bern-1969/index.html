September 18th, 2013

“When Attitudes Become Form” at Kunsthalle Bern, 1969

Artists: Carl Andre, Giovanni Anselmo, Richard Artschwager, Thomas Bang, Jared Bark, Robert Barry, Joseph Beuys, Alighiero Boetti, Mel Bochner, Marinus Boezem, Bill Bollinger, Michael Buthe, Pier Paolo Calzolari, Paul Cotton, Hanne Darboven, Walter de Maria, Jan Dibbets, Ger van Elk, Rafael Ferrer, Barry Flanagan, Ted Glass, Hans Haacke, Michael Heizer, Eva Hesse, Douglas Huebler, Paolo Icaro, Alain Jacquet, Neil Jenney, Stephen Kaltenbach, Jo Ann Kaplan, Edward Kienholz, Yves Klein, Joseph Kosuth, Jannis Kounellis, Gary B. Venue: Kunsthalle Bern

Exhibition Title: Live in Your Head: When Attitudes Become Form (Works – Concepts – Processes – Situations – Information)

Date: March 22 – April 27, 1969

Note: We’re publishing documentation of this exhibition as it was mounted in 1969 on the occasion of its reconstruction by Fondazione Prada at Ca’ Corner della Regina, Venice. You can view documentation of the reconstruction here.
