October 4th, 2009

“Scorpio’s Garden” at Temporare Kunsthalle Berlin

Artists: Monica Bonvicini, Enrico David, Jason Dodge, Elmgreen & Dragset, Isa Genzken, Julian Göthe, Karl Holmqvist, Alexandra Hopf, Judith Hopf, Laura Horelli, Anna-Kavata Mbiti, Shahryar Nashat, Henrik Olesen, Kirsten Pieroth, Lotte Reiniger, Dean Sameshima, Dash Snow, Josef Strau, Suzanne Treister, Susanne Winterling, Amelie von Wulffen

Venue: Temporare Kunsthalle Berlin

Exhibition Title: Scorpio’s Garden

Date: September 25 – November 15, 2009


Full gallery of images, press release and link available after the jump.

Press Release:

Selecting works and performances by more than 35 international artists for Scorpio’s Garden, Berlin-based Danish artist Kirstine Roepstorff gives a subjective snapshot of the Berlin art scene. With an unusual exhibition architecture, she creates new axes of vision and a special dynamics in the space. A spiral ramp and transparent curtains create spatial situations in which Roepstorff shows over 60 works, including sculptures and installations, but also drawings, collages, and film. The exhibition also includes a comprehensive performance and lecture series, beginning on the opening night and continuing on various Mondays during the exhibition.
