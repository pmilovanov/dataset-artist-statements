October 15th, 2017

Studio for Propositional Cinema Film Festival at Kestner Gesellschaft and Kunstverein Hannover

Artists: Anna-Sophie Berger, Henning Fehr and Philipp Rühr, Selina Grüter & Michèle Graf, Madeline Hollander, Sarah Kürten, Cally Spooner, Studio for Propositional Cinema, Alex Wissel & Jan Bonny

Venue: Kestner Gesellschaft and Kunstverein Hannover, Hannover (presented in conjunction with PRODUKTION. Made in Germany Drei at Kestner Gesellschaft, Kunstverein Hannover, Sprengel Museum Hannover)

Exhibition Title: On The Wall In Chalk Is Written: 2nd Studio for Propositional Cinema Film Festival

Organized by: Studio for Propositional Cinema

Date: September 1 – 3, 2017
