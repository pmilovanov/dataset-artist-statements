June 15th, 2015

Renaud Jerez at William Arnold

Artist: Renaud Jerez

Venue: William Arnold, New York

Exhibition Title: Home

Date: May 8 – July 3, 2015

Click here to view slideshow

Full gallery of images, press release and link available after the jump.

Images:















Images courtesy of William Arnold, New York

Press Release:

William Arnold is pleased to announce Home, a site-specific project by Renaud Jerez. On view from May 8 through July 3, 2015, it presents sculptures, paintings and site-specific works that stage the exhibition space, a private apartment in a new residential development in Brooklyn, NY, as a life-size puppet theatre.

Renaud Jerez (b. 1982, Narbonne, France) currently lives and works in Berlin. His work is currently exhibited in the New Museum Triennial. Recent solo exhibitions include GAMEC Bergamo, Italy; Autocenter, Berlin; Galerie Crèvecoeur, Paris, and M.O.T. International, London, among others. Recent group exhibitions include the K11 art museum, Shanghai; Palais de Tokyo, Paris; Grand Century, NY and Carlos/Ishikawa, London.
