March 14th, 2015

Group Show at 1857

Artists: Francis Alÿs, Alan Bean, Elaine Cameron-Weir, Mirak Jamal, Sean Landers and J. Parker Valentine, with a collection of objects curated by Markus Li Stensrud

Venue: 1857, Oslo

Exhibition Title: An Account of Discovery and Wonder; Detailing Intrepid Exploration, Inevitable Peril and Infallible Resolve, as Evidenced by Relics, Replicas, Artifacts, Counterfeits, Documents, Samples, Souvenirs, Ephemera, Scraps and Residue.

Date: February 2 – March 22, 2015
