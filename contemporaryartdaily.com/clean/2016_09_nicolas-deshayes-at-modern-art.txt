
Thames Water is Nicolas Deshayes’ first solo exhibition with Modern Art.
The exhibition comprises sculptures made of cast iron and hot water.
Nicolas Deshayes lives and works in London. He was born in Nancy, France, in 1983. He completed MA Sculpture at the Royal College of Art in 2009 and BA Sculpture at Chelsea College of Art and Design in 2005.
Deshayes’ work is currently touring as part of British Art Show 8. He is taking part in Production Show 2016-2018 at Eastside Projects, Birmingham.
