
Alex Zachary is pleased to present a solo exhibition with Judith Bernstein.
Judith Bernstein (b. 1942), a prolific New York artist for more than forty years, will present a large scale installation in the gallery. Her recent solo exhibitions recently include Mitchell Algus, New York (2008)and The Box, Los Angeles(2009); her work has also been seen recently in “Greater New York 2010,”MoMA/PS1; and “The Last Newspaper, ”New Museum, New York. Bernstein is included in the permanent collections of the Museum of Modern Art and the Whitney Museum of American Art among many others.
