
The exhibition focuses on a selection of works from late 2015. They are from an ongoing series of paintings in which the artist uses images captured from The New York Times website. They are made with an Epson 9900 printer, and the ink is UltraChrome HDR.
Wade Guyton (b. 1972, Hammond, IN) currently has a solo exhibition at the Musée d’art moderne et contemporain (MAMCO) in Geneva until January 29, 2017. His solo exhibition at the Museum Brandhorst in Munich opens on January 28, 2017 and will be on view until April 30th. The artist lives and works in New York.
